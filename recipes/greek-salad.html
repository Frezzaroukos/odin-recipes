Description

Greek salad, also known as "Horiatiki", is a refreshing and colorful dish that perfectly captures the essence of Mediterranean cuisine. It's an iconic part of Greek food culture that's enjoyed worldwide.

This simple yet flavorful salad combines fresh vegetables, feta cheese, and olives, dressed with olive oil and herbs for a delicious taste of Greece.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, cut into chunks
  • 1 large cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 200g feta cheese, cut into a block or crumbled
  • 16-20 Kalamata olives
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • Salt to taste
  • 4-5 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1-2 tablespoons red wine vinegar (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon capers (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and cut the tomatoes into wedges or chunks and place them in a large bowl.
  2. Peel the cucumber, cut in half lengthwise, remove the seeds with a spoon, and slice. Add to the bowl.
  3. Thinly slice the red onion and add to the mix.
  4. Remove the seeds from the green pepper, cut into rings or strips, and add to the bowl.
  5. Add the Kalamata olives and capers (if using).
  6. Place the block of feta cheese on top of the salad (traditional method) or crumble it over the vegetables.
  7. Sprinkle with dried oregano and salt to taste.
  8. Drizzle with olive oil and add a splash of red wine vinegar if desired.
  9. Serve immediately at room temperature with fresh bread.

Note: In an authentic Greek salad, the ingredients are not tossed together but arranged in the bowl, with the feta placed on top.
